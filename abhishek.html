ABOUT

The History Of NMEASE

NMEASE stands for Nayan Mehta – Excellence, Agility, Skills & Empowerment. We are a training and consulting organization dedicated to building stronger people, smarter processes, and sustainable performance for businesses of all sizes. At NMEASE, we believe training should do more than just transfer knowledge — it should transform people, processes, and performance. Our name sounds like “Enemies”, and that’s no coincidence. We are the enemies of inefficiency, bottlenecks, and mediocrity. We stand against slow processes, wasted potential, and degrowth. We are the allies of excellence, growth, and empowerment. With certified expertise in Lean Six Sigma, POSH, soft skills, leadership, and process excellence, we empower individuals to grow, organizations to scale, and businesses to transform. At NMEASE, we don’t just train — we Train to Transform.

MEET THE FOUNDER

NAYAN MEHTA - FOUNDER

Nayan Mehta – Company Secretary | Certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Trainer | POSH-Certified Trainer | Quality Assurance Expert Every transformation begins with a dream. For Nayan Mehta, that dream was to create a space where people could unlock their true potential, organizations could achieve excellence, and learning could spark lasting change. Having worked in diverse industries and global environments.

Nayan saw a common challenge: brilliant people and businesses held back by slow processes, unclear skills, and untapped potential. This realization became the seed of NMEASE. Armed with expertise as a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, certified Quality Assurance Manager, and POSH Trainer, Nayan combined process excellence with people development to build training programs that are practical, impactful, and transformational. Her vision is simple yet powerful: To be the enemy of inefficiency, bottlenecks, and mediocrity, and the ally of excellence, growth, and empowerment. Through NMEASE, Nayan continues her mission — not just to train people, but to Train to Transform lives, careers, and organizations.
